Teaching

"I would rather have questions that can't be answered than answers that can't be questioned."
- Richard Feynman

Teaching and mentorship are crucial to my development and growth as a scholar. My teaching philosophy has three objectives:

  • Exposing students to the cutting edge of political science research
  • Engaging with diverse student learning styles
  • Continuously improving my instruction to meet student needs

My hope is that in grounding my teaching with these objectives, I can inspire students to take ownership of their learning both in and beyond the classroom. Below is a sample of the courses that I have taught at the undergraduate and postgraduate level, which reflect my teaching philosophy.

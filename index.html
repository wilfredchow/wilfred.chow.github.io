Welcome

Greetings!

I am an assistant professor in the School of International Studies at the University of Nottingham Ningbo China. My research interests include international relations, political economy, public opinion, and methodology. Specifically, I explore the role of mass publics in international relations by drawing from a diverse set of fields such as comparative authoritarianism, identity politics, and political communication. I employ a multi-method approach that encompasses spatial econometrics, survey experiments, and automated text analysis.

If you would like more information about my work, please contact me.
